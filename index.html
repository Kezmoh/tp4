Lemon Panko Crusted Salmon

Photo by Omar

Ingredients


  • 4 large eggs, lightly beaten
  • 1 cup 2% milk
  • 2 tablespoons sugar
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • 1/8 teaspoon salt
  • 12 slices day-old sandwich bread
  • Optional toppings: Butter, maple syrup, fresh berries and confectioners' sugar

Kitchen Equipment


  • Pan/Griddle
  • Plate/Shallow dish

Directions


  1. In a shallow dish, whisk together the first 5 ingredients. Preheat a greased griddle over medium heat.
  2. Dip bread in egg mixture, allowing to soak 30 seconds on each side. Cook on griddle until golden brown on both sides. Serve with toppings as desired.
